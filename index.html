Plant Me

Da Viva's Plant Me paper bags are sustainable seed embedded paper bags. Before telling you about our product, let me tell you a fact. Using a single paper bag less than 4 times is said to be polluting the environment.
This project is a contribution to the Environment. Plant Me paper bags are far more strong with it's new design which increases it's strength to 10X. Plant me's unique design can hold objects which are 10 times heavier than normal paperbags can hold!. The Unique element that is added to increase the strenght is called as "Bridge".
Bridge is nothing but the zig zag patterned foldings of the paper. This bridge acts as a support and weight is evenly distributed, this is how the strenght of the paper bag is increased.
